Videos of people editing text

Videos of people editing text, explaining the shortcuts they use, and recordings of their keyboards as they work. The purpose of this page is to provide a reference for people writing new software which involves text editing tasks.

Contribute

I would be interested in adding your video to this page, provided that it demonstrates one of the following qualities which is not yet represented by another video:

  • A natural language not yet represented
  • An operating system not yet represented
  • A keyboard layout or design not yet represented
  • Software with novel editing features
  • A novel workflow, e.g. screen reader or dictation software

When preparing your video, please use a camera to capture a video of your keyboard. I recommend OBS to capture a recording of both your screen and keyboard, as well as audio from your microphone. Please narrate your workflow in English, and include details which seem "obvious" to you; they might not be obvious to someone else. Contact Drew DeVault <sir@cmpwn.com> to list your video. Please include the following details:

  • Your name, for attribution
  • The natural language you are demonstrating input for
  • Your keyboard model and layout
  • Your operating system and the names & version numbers of your software

Thank you!
